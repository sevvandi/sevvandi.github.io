Graphons are graph limits. They can be used as a generative model for dense graphs. A graphon is a symmetric, measureable function defined as $W:[0.1]^2 \to [0,1]$. However, graphons can only generate dense graphs. This set of papers extend graphons to generate sparse graphs.

Graphons of line graphs

This paper shows that for a subset of sparse graphs, their line graphs are dense. As such, modeling the graphon of line graphs is useful for a subset of sparse graphs.
